good place to visit


  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark in Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and thousands of other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture with rich history.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Known for its panoramic city views and unique white dome.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A world-renowned avenue for luxury shopping and grand city strolls.
  • Croissants – A classic French pastry known for its buttery layers and flakiness.
  • Coq au Vin – A traditional dish of chicken braised with wine, mushrooms, and garlic.
  • Baguette – The iconic French bread, enjoyed best when fresh and crusty.
  • Ratatouille – A flavorful vegetable stew from Provence, packed with seasonal ingredients.
  • Crème Brûlée – A rich custard dessert with a caramelized sugar crust.
